# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard 

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Superstore Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI & Excel to analyze business performance across revenue, profit, and sales trends.

The dashboard provides a **comprehensive overview of sales performance**, enabling stakeholders to monitor key metrics, identify growth patterns, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to:
* Track revenue and profit performance efficiently
* Understand regional sales distribution
* Identify high-performing product categories
* Monitor growth trends over time

This dashboard addresses these challenges by transforming raw sales data into **clear, actionable insights**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
* Analyze overall sales and profitability
* Track sales and profit growth over time
* Understand regional sales distribution
* Identify performance by category and segment
* Evaluate quarterly sales trends

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue:** $12.64M
* **Total Profit:** $1.47M
* **Profit Margin:** 11.6%
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 178K
* **Average Order Value:** $505

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Sales Performance Tracking

* Revenue and profit growth over time
* Quarterly sales trend analysis

### 🔹 Regional Analysis

* Sales distribution across regions (West, East, North, South)

### 🔹 Product Insights

* Profit margin by category
* Sales breakdown by segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* 📈 Sales and profit show **consistent growth over time**
* 🌍 The **West region dominates sales (52%)**
* 💻 **Technology category has the highest profit margin**
* 🪑 Furniture shows **lower profitability compared to other categories**
* 📊 Consumer segment contributes the largest share of sales
