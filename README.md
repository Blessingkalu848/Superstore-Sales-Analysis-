# Key insights-
- The West region generated the highest sales (52%), making it the top-performing region.
- Technology category has the highest profit margin (~14%).
- Sales and profit show consistent growth from 2011 to 2014.
- Consumer segment contributes the highest revenue among all segments.
- Furniture has the lowest profit margin (~6.9%), indicating potential inefficiencies.
- # Recommendation
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions like the West.
- Improve profitability in the Furniture category.
- Invest more in Technology products due to high margins.
